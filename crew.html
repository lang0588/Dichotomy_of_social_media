Our Crew

Beyond the Screen is produced by three passionate student filmmakers at Algonquin College: director/editor Misty Mett, producer Marielle Garcia, and cinematographer Carter Burgess.

Misty Mett: @mmisty.media

A lifetime multimedia artist, 22-year-old Canadian-American Misty Mett has always had an interest in storytelling and the human condition. Now studying Film and Media Production at Algonquin College, she aspires to be a director and writer, but a filmmaker above all else.

Marielle Garcia: @ellegarciafilms

As a passionate 18-year-old film student, Marielle Garcia has always had a love for storytelling. She'll most likely be found hanging out with friends, getting lost in a soundtrack, or diving into anything that sparks her curiosity. She's always open to learning and growing, bringing creativity and enthusiasm to everything she does.

Carter Burgess: @carter___burgess

Carter Burgess is 20 years old with a strong passion for hockey, golf, film, video games, and fitness. With deep appreciation for teamwork and discipline developed through years on the ice, he's constantly striving for growth both personally and professionally.
